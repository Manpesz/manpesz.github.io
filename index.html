About Manpesz

Manpesz is a youth-led independent initiative focused on the design, engineering, and simulation of innovative commercial aircraft. Our mission is to design affordable, reliable, and efficient regional aircraft, inspired by projects like the Airbus A320 and recent developments by Chinese manufacturers.

We combine passion, technology, and creativity to build the next generation of aircraft suited for emerging markets and challenging environments.

Current Project – A320-Inspired Narrow Body

Our latest project is a cost-effective, simplified alternative to the Airbus A320, focused on:

  • Lightweight, robust airframe optimized for low-cost manufacturing
  • Modern glass cockpit with side-stick controls
  • Efficient and maintainable fly-by-wire systems
  • Optimized for regional routes with ~150 passengers
